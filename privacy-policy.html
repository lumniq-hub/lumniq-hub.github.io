Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 26, 2025

MedMate is a medication reminder and health tracking app. We take your health data seriously. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use MedMate:

Account Information

  • Email address and password (if you sign in with email)
  • Google account name and email (if you sign in with Google)
  • Anonymous user ID (if you use the app without signing in)

Health & Medication Data

  • Medication names, dosages, schedules, and instructions you enter
  • Dose history (taken, missed, snoozed)
  • Caregiver relationships and shared profiles
  • Blood glucose, blood pressure, and body weight — only if you connect Android Health Connect and grant permission

Device & Usage Data

  • App usage events (screens visited, features used) via Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Device type and OS version (collected automatically by Firebase)

Camera

  • Camera access is used only for the Pill Identifier feature to scan medication labels. Photos are not stored or uploaded.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver medication reminders and dose notifications on schedule
  • To sync your data across devices when you are signed in
  • To enable the caregiver sharing feature
  • To display your health vitals alongside your medication history
  • To identify and fix crashes and improve app stability
  • To understand which features are used so we can improve the app

3. How We Store Your Data

Your account and medication data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, protected by Firebase Authentication. Only you (and caregivers you explicitly invite) can access your data.

Health vitals from Android Health Connect are read directly on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only in these limited cases:

  • Caregivers you invite: Medication and dose data is shared with caregivers you explicitly add inside the app. You can revoke access at any time.
  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication, database, analytics, and crash reporting. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by Firebase.
  • U.S. National Library of Medicine (NLM) API: Medication names are sent to the RxNav API to check for drug interactions. No personal identifiers are included.
  • U.S. FDA API: Medication names are sent to the OpenFDA API for the pill identifier feature. No personal identifiers are included.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & export: You can view all your medication and dose data inside the app at any time.
  • Delete your account: You can delete your account from the Settings screen. This permanently removes your data from our servers within 30 days.
  • Health Connect: You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time through Android Settings → Health Connect.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications at any time through Android Settings → Apps → MedMate.
  • Analytics opt-out: You can opt out of analytics collection in the app's Settings screen.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained longer.

7. Children's Privacy

MedMate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules to protect your data. Access is restricted to authenticated users only. We use HTTPS for all data in transit.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of MedMate after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
