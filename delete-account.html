Delete Your Account

Developed by Lumniq Studio

You can delete your MedMate account and all associated data directly inside the app — no need to contact support.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

  1. Open the MedMate app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete account (shown in red).
  5. Review the list of data that will be deleted, then tap Continue.
  6. Confirm by tapping Delete forever.

Deletion is immediate. Once confirmed, your account and all data are permanently removed — there is no recovery option.

What Gets Deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of the following with no retention period:

  • Your Firebase authentication account
  • All medications and schedules
  • All dose history and adherence logs
  • All refill logs
  • All symptom entries
  • All profile data
This action cannot be undone. Export or note any data you want to keep before deleting your account.

What Is Not Deleted

  • Anonymous crash reports — crash data collected by Firebase Crashlytics does not contain personally identifiable information and is retained for debugging purposes per Google's data retention policy.
  • Aggregated analytics — non-identifiable usage statistics collected by Firebase Analytics are retained per Google's policy.

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to sign in to your account to use the in-app deletion flow, contact us and we will delete your account manually within 30 days.
